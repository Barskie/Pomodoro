Frequently Asked Questions

How can I gain access to Pivot's web application features?

It is required for the users to have a registered account to get full access to Pivot's features. If you haven't registered already, just click on the avatar dropdown in the navigation bar (the dropdown icon in the top right corner of the page), then click the SIGN-UP option, so that you will be redirected to the sign-up page.

How can I register an account?

In the landing page you can click the "GET STARTED" button so that you can be directed to the homepage where you can click on any of the registered user level features, so that a pop-up window that will require you to log-in or sign-up will appear. You can also click on the avatar dropdown in the menu navigation bar, then click the SIGN-UP option, so that you will be redirected to the sign-up page. Once in the sign-up page, enter your information in the provided fields, make sure to use a valid email for the registration, and to use a unique username and a complex password. Remember to also read thoroughly the terms and conditions. Once you submit your registration head to your email to verify your account, when done, you can now log-in to your account and gain access to Pivot's features.

What can I do if I lose access to my account?

When you lost your access to your account, you can use the "Forgot Password" feature to gain it back. Just enter your registered email address then wait to recieve the email. Once recieved, open the link, then input a new password then confirm it once again to match the one you entered in the previous field. If it matches, then upon submitting, the password for the account would be changed.

What is the set default time for the pomodoro timer and the break timer?

The default countdown of the POMODORO timer is 25 minutes, and for the BREAK timer it is 5 minutes. This can be customizable, however, the set time for the break timer should not exceed the set time of the pomodoro timer.

What can I do in Pivot's timer page?

You can start and stop the timer, skip the session afterwards, customize it, rename its session name, and add more sessions to it or set the number of pomodoro sessions that you want to accomplish. You can also add notes when you expand a specific session, and also play music in the embedded spotify playlist. You can easily view your completed sessions, and save the session as a routine which will be saved in the Routines page.

What can I do in Pivot's To-do page?

You can list down your tasks in the to-do list page, group or create group task, and turn them into sessions.

What can I do in Pivot's Routines page?

Your saved routines will be stored and accessible in this page, you can also create your own routines directly into this page. Once you choose one of your created or saved routines, you can view, edit, delete, or start the routine with the sessions under it.

What can I do in Pivot's Calendar page?

You can set and view your schedule here, from your very own google calendar, since google calendar is integrated in Pivot.

What are the music available in the embedded spotify playlist

The only available music in the embedded spotify playlist feature is classical music, because based from Team Pivot's research, classical music can heighten your focus. Which is why we highly recommend you to use it.

Will I be able to track my productivity with Pivot?

Yes, a weekly report is made available for each individual registered users, for them to keep track of their performance. It is highly recommended for the users to answer the Time Management Assessment Questionnare which will be given every after 4 pomodoro sessions, for a much accurate report.
