POMODORO TECHNIQUE

Pomodoro Technique is a tool in helping them improve their focus which would lead to increase in their productivity. With their tasks divided into chunks of time, they would be able to focus on finishing their goal within the 25 minute timeframe for productivity (Scroggs, 2020).

HOW TO USE POMODORO

There would be a 25 minute session that would be followed with a 5 minute break. A productive morning can be represented by four Pomodoro sessions. It's amazing how much you can accomplish in brief periods of concentrated effort. To determine the effectiveness of the Pomodoro Technique the user must have 4 successful pomodoros (Collins B, 2020).
