POMODORO TECHNIQUE

Pomodoro Technique is a tool that helps a person in improving their focus which would lead to increase in their productivity. With their tasks divided into chunks of time, they would be able to focus on finishing their goal within the 25 minute timeframe for productivity (Scroggs, 2020).

HOW TO USE POMODORO

  • First, identify your tasks for the day.
  • Use Pivot's Pomodoro Timer while doing your tasks, which is set to 25 minites in default or 1 Pomodoro Session.
  • Focus on your work. You can turn on some music with Pivot's embedded spotify playlist to help and accompany you while working.
  • Once you completed 1 pomodoro session, take a 5 minute break.
  • After 4 Pomodoro Sessions, take a break and an assessment to evaluate your productivity.
